# Session 001: Supplement Gummy Market Research

**Date:** 2026-04-06
**Status:** Complete

## Summary

Researched the US supplement gummy market for comparison against the cannabis/hemp functional gummy market ($4.5B-$7.1B). Gathered data from multiple market research firms on total market size, functional segments (sleep, stress, energy, focus), growth rates, key players, and competitive overlap.

## Key Findings

- **US supplement gummy market:** ~$4.2B-$5.5B (2024), depending on source and scope definition
- **Global supplement gummy market:** ~$10.5B-$10.7B (2024)
- **Growth rate:** 9-15% CAGR depending on source
- **Key players:** Vitafusion (Church & Dwight), OLLY (Unilever), ZzzQuil (P&G), Nature Made (Otsuka), Goli
- **Cannabis/hemp gummy market (CBD alone):** $4.75B globally in 2024, growing at 30.8% CAGR -- much faster than supplement gummies
- **Key insight:** The cannabis/hemp functional gummy market ($4.5B-$7.1B) is roughly comparable in size to the US supplement gummy market ($4.2B-$5.5B), which is notable given supplements are a mature, established category

## Functional Segments Researched

- Sleep gummies (melatonin): ~$450M-$1.3B depending on scope
- Stress/calm gummies: ~$500M-$2.2B depending on scope
- Energy gummies: ~$1.6B globally
- Focus/cognitive gummies: subset of $3.56B US brain health supplements market
