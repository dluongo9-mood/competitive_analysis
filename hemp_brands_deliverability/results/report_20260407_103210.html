Availability Ratings Explained

Each brand is tested using two methods: (1) scraping the shipping policy page for state restriction language, and (2) adding a THCA product to cart and attempting checkout with an OH or TX zip code. The final verdict combines both signals.

Yes Confirmed deliverable. Checkout flow completed successfully — the site accepted the zip code and displayed shipping rates or a delivery estimate. The product can be ordered to this state.
Likely Yes Probably deliverable, not fully confirmed. The shipping policy lists restricted states but this state is not among them, or no restriction language was found at all. However, the cart/checkout flow could not be completed (e.g., site uses a non-standard checkout, no THCA product found, or age gate blocked the flow), so we could not get a definitive checkout confirmation.
Unclear Insufficient data. Neither the shipping policy nor the checkout flow produced a clear signal. This typically means no shipping policy page was found and the cart/checkout flow failed before reaching the shipping step. Manual verification recommended.
Likely No Probably restricted. The shipping policy mentions this state alongside restriction keywords (e.g., “shipping restrictions”) but the exact context is ambiguous — it may be a partial restriction or product-specific rule rather than a blanket ban. Checkout was not completed to confirm.
No Confirmed restricted. The checkout page explicitly blocked the order — either showing a “cannot ship to this state” message or the shipping policy explicitly names this state on a restricted list with clear “do not ship” language.
